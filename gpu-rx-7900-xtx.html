Graphics Card

AMD Radeon RX 7900 XTX

RDNA 3 raster powerhouse with DP 2.1 futureproofing

AMD's flagship uses chiplet design and second-gen Infinity Cache to deliver outstanding raster performance with support for DisplayPort 2.1 monitors.

Explore other graphics cards

Why it leads right now

24 GB of 20 Gbps GDDR6 and Fluid Motion Frames keep ultra-wide and 4K 165 Hz panels fed without sacrificing detail.

The card stays cooler than the 4090 in reference form and benefits from aggressive undervolts that trim 50–70 W without impacting performance.

Performance highlights

  • USB-C DisplayPort 2.1 drives 8K 165 Hz next-gen displays.
  • Supports FSR 3 with frame generation on any GPU workflow.
  • SmartAccess Memory pairing with Ryzen 7000 nets up to 8% extra performance.

Key specifications

  • ArchitectureRDNA 3
  • Stream Processors6144
  • Game Clock2.3 GHz
  • Memory24 GB GDDR6 20 Gbps
  • Total Board Power355 W
  • Outputs2x DisplayPort 2.1, 1x HDMI 2.1, 1x USB-C

Best suited for

Builders chasing high-refresh raster performance and DisplayPort 2.1 connectivity.

Recommended pairings

  • Seasonic Prime TX-1000Titanium efficiency with hybrid fan control.
  • AMD Ryzen 9 7950X3DSMART Access Memory synergy for extra frames.

Buyer checklist

  • Budget for a 360 mm radiator or quality air intake—the XTX dumps significant heat.
  • Use Radeon Software's undervolt tool to drop temps while keeping stock performance.
  • Keep driver firmware updated for FSR 3 titles rolling out through 2025.
