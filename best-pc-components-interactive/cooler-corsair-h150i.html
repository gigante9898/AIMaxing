Cooling

Corsair iCUE Link H150i LCD

360 mm liquid cooler with smart chainable ecosystem

Corsair's latest H150i integrates daisy-chainable fans, an IPS pump cap display, and refined pump internals for stronger thermal performance and easier cable routing.

Explore other cooling solutions

Why it leads right now

The second-gen pump uses a split-flow cold plate to improve coolant distribution, while pre-applied XTM70 paste removes guesswork during installation.

Link ecosystem support lets builders synchronize lighting, telemetry, and fan curves through a single hub.

Performance highlights

  • IPS 2.1" display shows temps, GIFs, or system stats in 24-bit color.
  • Zero RPM mode keeps fans silent during idle or light workloads.
  • Tool-free Slide-Lock radiator fittings speed up fan swaps.

Key specifications

  • Radiator Size360 mm (397 x 120 x 27 mm)
  • Fans3x 120 mm AF RGB Link
  • Speed Range550 – 2100 RPM
  • Noise Level5 – 34.1 dB(A)
  • CompatibilityIntel LGA1700/1200 & AMD AM5/AM4

Best suited for

High-wattage Intel or AMD chips that benefit from liquid cooling and visual flair.

Recommended pairings

  • Intel Core i9-14900KKeeps the hybrid processor under 90°C during AVX loads.
  • Corsair RM1000x SHIFTChain iCUE Link power and lighting for tidy cable runs.

Buyer checklist

  • Mount the radiator top-exhaust when possible to avoid pump gurgle.
  • Connect all Link accessories to a single hub for clean cable management.
  • Run the burn-in wizard in iCUE to calibrate fan curves after filling your loop.
