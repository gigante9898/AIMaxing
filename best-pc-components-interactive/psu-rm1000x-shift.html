Power Supply

Corsair RM1000x SHIFT

ATX 3.0-ready PSU with side-mounted modular interface

Corsair reimagined cable management by moving the modular panel to the side, making the RM1000x SHIFT a dream for modern airflow-focused cases while delivering rock-solid power delivery.

Explore other power supplies

Why it leads right now

Native 12VHPWR and 16-pin Sense wires handle Ada-class GPUs without adapters, while 105°C-rated capacitors ensure longevity.

Zero RPM fan mode keeps the unit silent under everyday workloads, only ramping up once power draw exceeds roughly 400 W.

Performance highlights

  • Meets full ATX 3.0 transient specs for 2x GPU power excursions.
  • Cypress 750 microcontroller manages precise voltage regulation.
  • Ten-year warranty backed by Corsair's global support network.

  • Efficiency80 PLUS Gold

Best suited for

High-wattage gaming builds that need tidy cabling and ATX 3.0 compliance.

Recommended pairings

  • NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090Provides reliable 12VHPWR power headroom.
  • Intel Core i9-14900KPlenty of EPS capacity for heavy overclocks.

Buyer checklist

  • Leave 35 mm clearance on the PSU shroud side to route cables comfortably.
  • Use the included Sense 0/1 cable for accurate GPU power reporting.
  • Run Corsair iCUE monitoring via supported motherboards for live telemetry.
