Samsung 990 PRO 2TB

Workhorse PCIe 4.0 NVMe drive with top-tier endurance

Samsung's 990 PRO uses an in-house Pascal controller and 7th-gen V-NAND to deliver excellent sustained writes and an optional heatsink variant for compact builds.

Explore other storage picks

Why it leads right now

Firmware refinements in 2024 restored peak random write performance, making it ideal for OS, game, and scratch disk duties.

Magician software adds automatic firmware updates, secure erase, and performance profiles for creators swapping between workloads.

  • Heatsink variant fits PlayStation 5 and compact motherboards alike.
  • 5-year warranty with 1200 TBW endurance rating on the 2 TB model.
