G.Skill Trident Z5 Neo DDR5-6000

EXPO-tuned DDR5 kit that nails the AM5 sweet spot

Trident Z5 Neo pairs binned Hynix M-die ICs with a matte stealth heatspreader, delivering DDR5-6000 CL30 performance validated on AMD EXPO profiles.

Explore other memory kits

Why it leads right now

This dual-rank kit keeps Infinity Fabric sync happy while staying cool under load, making it the go-to choice for Ryzen 7000 builders.

G.Skill's lifetime warranty and consistent binning mean fewer surprises during first boot and memory training.

Performance highlights

  • Preloaded EXPO I & II profiles for one-click setup on X670E/B650E boards.
  • Tight timings (CL30-38-38) boost real-world latency-sensitive workloads.
  • Low-profile design clears most dual-tower air coolers.
