Motherboard

ASUS ROG Crosshair X670E Hero

AM5 halo board with polished firmware and PCIe 5.0 lanes

Crosshair X670E Hero blends 18+2 teamed power stages, dual USB4 ports, and AI overclocking features, making it the reference AM5 board for Ryzen flagships.

Explore other motherboards

Why it leads right now

You get dual PCIe 5.0 M.2 slots, a bundled Gen5 add-in card, and shielded DDR5 slots rated for EXPO 6400+, ideal for builders who need storage flexibility.

The BIOS includes per-core Curve Optimizer presets for X3D processors and fan controls granular enough for complex custom loops.

Performance highlights

  • Robust 18+2+2 power design with premium heatsinks keeps VRM temps below 60°C.
  • Two USB4 40 Gbps ports with PD 3.0 support for high-speed external workflows.
  • Q-Release PCIe latch and tool-free M.2 clips simplify iterative testing.

Key specifications

  • Form FactorATX
  • ChipsetAMD X670E
  • Memory Support4x DDR5 up to 6400+ (OC)
  • Expansion2x PCIe 5.0 x16 (x8/x8)
  • Storage2x PCIe 5.0 M.2 + 2x PCIe 4.0 M.2
  • NetworkingIntel 2.5G LAN + Wi-Fi 6E

Best suited for

AM5 overclockers and creators needing reliable PCIe 5.0 support.

Recommended pairings

  • AMD Ryzen 9 7950X3DOptimized BIOS profiles for X3D chips.
  • Samsung 990 PRO 2TBFully saturates PCIe 4.0 lanes with reliable thermals.

Buyer checklist

  • Flash the latest BIOS via USB FlashBack before first boot to ensure CPU support.
  • Populate the top M.2 slot with a Gen5 drive if you need maximum scratch disk speed.
  • Leverage ASUS AI Cooling II to calibrate fan curves after initial build.
