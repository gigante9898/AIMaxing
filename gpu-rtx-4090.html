Graphics Card

NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090

Ada Lovelace flagship for uncompromised 4K and creation

RTX 4090 combines 16384 CUDA cores, 4th-gen Tensor cores, and DLSS 3 frame generation to push triple-digit frame rates in 4K with path tracing enabled.

Explore other graphics cards

Why it leads right now

The AD102 GPU offers a massive 72 MB L2 cache and 24 GB of 21 Gbps GDDR6X, enabling unparalleled performance in professional CUDA, OptiX, and AI workloads.

NVIDIA Studio drivers and dual AV1 encoders make it the go-to GPU for Unreal Engine developers, Blender artists, and high-end streamers.

Performance highlights

  • DLSS 3 and Frame Generation multiply performance in supported titles without sacrificing detail.
  • Stable 700+ W transient handling when matched with ATX 3.0 PSUs.
  • Creator-focused SDKs like Omniverse and Broadcast come free with GeForce Experience.
