Corsair Dominator Titanium DDR5-7200

Hand-binned DDR5 built for extreme XMP profiles

Corsair's Dominator Titanium leverages DHX cooling and premium ICs to sustain DDR5-7200 CL34 performance, aligning perfectly with Intel 14th Gen controllers.

Explore other memory kits

Why it leads right now

Integrated Corsair iCUE Link headers allow daisy-chained RGB control without clutter, and the kit ships with swappable top plates for styling.

Robust PMIC and on-board temperature sensors keep voltage ripple in check when chasing leaderboard memory OCs.
